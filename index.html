Pioneering Sustainable Polymer Solutions Since 2010.

At Sri Sai Polymer, we have been shaping the future of plastics for over 15 years. Founded in 2010, our commitment lies in delivering high-grade recycled plastic granules and chips that align with global quality benchmarks. With a focus on innovation, consistency, and eco-responsibility, we transform plastic waste into valuable raw materials that power industries worldwide. By blending advanced technology with sustainable practices, we ensure every product supports durability, reliability, and a cleaner environment. From standard supplies to tailored solutions, Sri Sai Polymer is your trusted partner in responsible plastic recycling and manufacturing.
